The Thesis

Anyone can generate.
Few can create.

We are at an inflection point. A single prompt can spin up an app, write a business plan, or generate a year's worth of content. The bottleneck is no longer capability — it's judgment.

The breakout AI companies and products of this era won't be those with the most compute or the fastest iteration cycles. They will be the ones built with taste — a rare combination of sharp decision-making, intentional distribution, and genuine delight for the people using them.

What Is Taste?

Taste is not aesthetic preference. It's a discipline — the accumulated weight of thousands of good decisions.

Decision-making

What to build, what to cut, and — crucially — what to say no to. Taste lives in the edit, not the draft.

Distribution

How a product finds its people. Great products are not discovered — they are placed, with intention, in front of the right audience at the right moment.

Delight

The small, unreasonable details that make users feel seen. Delight is what turns a tool into something people talk about.

The Show

Art of AI is a podcast and media collective for the product builders, designers, operators, engineers, and founders who refuse to ship mediocre.

Each episode is a candid conversation with the people actually doing it — the ones obsessing over the details, rethinking the defaults, and making AI products that feel human.

We cover craft. We go deep on the decisions that shaped a product, the distribution bets that paid off, and the moments of delight that made users stay.
